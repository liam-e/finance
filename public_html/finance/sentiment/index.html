WallStreetBets stock tracker

Last updated

This website scans Reddit posts on WallStreetBets to track how many times stock tickers are mentioned and the sentiment of the post. The website is currently set to collect the newest posts every 15 minutes.

All frequency is measured by percentage of posts that mention the stock out of all new posts, and is plotted logarithmically. A python library called nltk is used to determine the sentiment of posts that mention a stock, and gives number between 1 and -1, with one being very positive and -1 being very negative. Neutral sentiment (values between 0.3 and -0.3) are ignored.

Graphs are updated every 6 hours New Zealand time. All times are currently displayed in New Zealand time.

Some text displayed may be mistaken for stock tickers. Be aware that bots are included in the data.

The information contained on this website is not intended as, and should not be construed as financial advice.
