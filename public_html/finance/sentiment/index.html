Reddit stock sentiment tracker

This website scans Reddit posts on Wallstreetbets to track how many times stock tickers are mentioned and the sentiment.

Graphs are updated at midday New Zealand time. All times are currently displayed in New Zealand time.

Some text displayed may be mistaken for stock tickers.

The information contained on this website is not intended as, and should not be construed as financial advice. Use at your own risk.
